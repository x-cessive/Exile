###### INSTALLATION GUIDE

Copy VEMFr_client folder to the root of your missiom pbo  (altis.pbo or whatever map you use.)

Add the content of the init.sqf to init.sqf in your mission pbo.

Add the content of the description.ext to description.ext in your mission pbo.

Copy a3_vemf_reloaded.pbo to  @ExileServer\addons\ OR @epochhive\addons\

Copy a3_vemf_reloaded_config.pbo to @ExileServer\addons\ OR @epochhive\addons\


To configure vemf open config.cpp in a3_vemf_reloaded.pbo



###### CONFIGURATION GUIDE
For the sake of comfort, VEMFr uses a separate .pbo to store -and use- settings.
All default settings are stored inside a3_vemf_reloaded.pbo by default. If you want to change a certain setting, then use a3_vemf_reloaded_config\config.cpp for that. It will save you a lot of time. You are welcome.
